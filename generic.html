About Me

Coming from a science and entrepreneurship background, I've learned to breakdown information thoroughly and uniquely. My science background in Biology and Computer Science taught me to analyze and interpretate information correctly. My entrepreneurship background taught me to be pragmatic and confident in relaying information, whether to small groups or to hundreds of individuals.

My interest in Data stems from my desire to face more challenges in my work. While I love biology, the routineness of labwork helped me realize that I want a career that's driven by intellect and reasoning, rather than constant reiteration. What I find most appealing about the data industry is its ever-growing and changing nature. I want to be able to face unique problems and come to understand them through research and reasoning.

I'm excited to continue my journey in Data, as the opportunity to learn seems ever present. To date I've completed a computer science minor and IBM's Data Analyst Professional certificate, but I look forward to working with a great team who can help me expand my knowledge even further.
